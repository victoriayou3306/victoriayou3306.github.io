Cranes

I make paper cranes. I don't have a clear reason why— it just became a thing when I got stressed or bored in high school. Now I fold them whenever my hands are free, and it usually helps me think/focus.

*I almost exclusively use old scratch paper so its not totally wasteful.

I give most of my cranes to my friend Anshika. Every single one is counted before I go home or mail them to Alabama. There are probably a few thousand more that I haven't counted or lost along the way. This page only really exists for me to keep count, although I do have some crane-inspired projects in progress.
