Projects

I've worked on a wide variety of projects, depending on the situation. I didn't include many class projects unless it was a major focus of mine or I did a lot of additional work after the assignment was submitted.

My work at KLA happens to be a perfect intersection of the two in precision motion, and my personal projects display much of my side hobbies.

*Unsurprising outcome for a kid who spent Covid with a 3D Printer and too much time in isolation.
